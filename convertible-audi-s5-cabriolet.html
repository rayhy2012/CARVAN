Product Description

Air out your feelings

Accelerating the senses. Top-down driving finds a higher gear. The Audi S5 Cabriolet.

There isn't a wrong way to see the right angles

Whichever the perspective, it’s easy for the eyes to follow the flowing lines of the Audi S5 Cabriolet. On the Audi S5 Cabriolet, sculpted air intakes on the front bumper, a sport-influenced rear diffuser and aluminum-optic paint accents serve as indicators of increased performance.

Overstated understatement

There’s a refinement about understated elegance that exudes luxury and tasteful design. In the Audi S5 Cabriolet, front S sport seats and a flat-bottom, three-spoke multi-function steering wheel add sporting flair to the atmosphere.

A seismic shift in connectivity

Inside the Audi S5 Cabriolet is a world of connected services. The digital screens of the available Audi Virtual cockpit and Audi MMI® touch display system, along with navigation with Google Maps®, can help you get up to date while you’re on the go.

Attention paid is priceless

To help the driver become aware of conditions surrounding the vehicle, the Audi S5 Cabriolet is equipped with driver assistance systems that can alert the driver of a potential incident, and, in some cases, can initiate braking, and steering application as preventive and counter measures.
