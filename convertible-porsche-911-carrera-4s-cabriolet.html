Product Description

Timeless design

A timeless design, a contemporary interpretation. The unmistakable silhouette of the 911 is characterised by its iconic flyline. It has barely changed since 1963, and has shaped the DNA of all Porsche models. Including, of course,
the 911 Cabriolet and Targa models.

Sports car performance

Powerful twin-turbo six-cylinder horizontally opposed engine in the rear. High-precision Porsche Doppelkupplung (PDK) transmission. Systems for increasing the driving dynamic.

Comfort

Power athlete. Design icon. Everyday hero. The 911 combines the power of a sports car with great interior comfort and the latest technology to make your everyday life easier.

Assistance systems

Numerous assistance systems, such as Porsche InnoDrive and Remote ParkAssist, provide support while driving and during everyday life.

Interior comfort

High-resolution screens, a clearly structured display concept and touch-sensitive buttons. In short, sports car ergonomics connected to the 21st century. Thanks to the intuitive Porsche Advanced Cockpit control concept, the interior is clearer and more dynamic than ever.
