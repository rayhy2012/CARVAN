Product Description

Pure Aerodynamics and Design

The 911 GT2 RS design reconciles diametrically opposing requirements such as maximum speed and drag coefficient and maximum downforce race-optimized aerodynamics, which is no easy task.
The front apron’s aerodynamic shape ensures optimum cooling. The extra air outlet at the hood front helps in providing airflow to the central radiator while deflecting airflow to provide aerodynamic downforce at the axle’s front.

Lightweight Construction

It’s made out of the lightest functional materials, which results in consistent lightweight construction to the very last detail, with the power-to-weight ratio being 2.58 kg/kW (4.63 lb/hp) which allows outstanding performance.

Wheels

The Porsche 911 GT2 RS has 20-inch wheels in the front axle and 21-inch wheels on its rear axle.
What does this mean? It means big, wide wheels, large tire contact area, great driving dynamics.

Titanium Rear Silencers

The Porsche 911 GT2 RS has newly-developed, independent exhaust system with titanium rear silencers and the throaty sound of a high-performance turbo.

Rear-Axle Steering

Rear-axle steering in the Porsche 911 GT2 RS has its advantages. At low speeds, it makes tight corners, turning in a circle and parking easier.
