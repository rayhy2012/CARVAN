Product Description

Design

With a new S-Class, the sun rises on a new era for the automobile. More thoughtful of its passengers. More in tune with its driver. More protective of those within or even near its elegant presence.

More evolved. Keeps evolving

The sensuous cabin is functional and feature-rich. Standard heated front seats, a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and 10.25-inch center screen harmonize with five options in hand-finished wood

Recognizes you. Remembers your likes

A 12.8-inch Organic LED (OLED) touchscreen is the centerpiece of up to five screens that can share content or entertain individually. You can personalize 800 settings, in the car or from your phone, in up to seven user profiles

Front air bags. Even in back

A perennial pioneer in occupant protection, the S-Class looks out for its own and even those around it with fresh advancements in safety. Among them, the benefits of front air bags can now be extended to the rear cabin

Easier in here. Safer out there

A more advanced suite of driver assists is standard. Systems and sensors network, look further ahead and into adjacent lanes, and tap into GPS and real-time data for better anticipation, quicker reaction, and smoother adaptation
